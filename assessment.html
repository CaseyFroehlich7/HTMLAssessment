Penn State Nittany Lion Wrestling Program

The Pennsylvania State University (Penn State or PSU) is a public state-related land-grant research university with campuses and facilities throughout Pennsylvania. Founded in 1855 as the Farmers' High School of Pennsylvania, Penn State became the state's only land-grant university in 1863. Today, Penn State is a major research university which conducts teaching, research, and public service. The Penn State wrestling program was established in 1909 and was declared national dual meet champion in 1921. Since then, Penn State has won 10 National Championship Titles. Nine of those titles have been won under the regime of head coach, Cael Sanderson, since arriving at Penn State in 2009.
